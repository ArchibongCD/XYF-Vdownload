Troubleshooting

"Video not found" error

This usually means:

  • The video link is incorrect or incomplete
  • The video has been deleted or made private
  • The video is region-restricted

Solution: Double-check the link and ensure the video is publicly accessible.

Download is slow or stuck
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try a lower video quality
  • Close other tabs or applications using bandwidth
  • Try again later if the platform is experiencing issues
"Platform not supported" message

We currently support YouTube, X (Twitter), and Facebook only. Other platforms will be added in future updates.

Video quality options are limited

Available quality options depend on what the source platform provides. Not all videos are available in HD.
